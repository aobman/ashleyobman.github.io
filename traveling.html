Traveling

For me, traveling opens a world of exploring new cultures and perspectives. From the never-ending White Sand dunes to the crystal clear waters in St. Kitts, the memories I've made are unforgettable. My favorite places so far have been Sedona, AZ (pictured on the left) and Oahu, HI (pictured on the right).

Although, it's quite impossible to pick true favorites. Colorado has it's own unique beauty, from blue lakes to abundant hot springs.

Lastly, who could pass up a snorkeling opportunity? The ocean is a whole other world. Below is me holding a sand dollar found in Cozumel waters!
